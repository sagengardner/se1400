Website Summary

What content will the site contain?

This sight will contain updates and news articles on college gymnastics. This will include stories on all D1 teams accross the country including individual gymnasts and meet results.

Where will you get all of the content for your site (ie. images & text)?

I will get my content for this website from watching college gymnastics. As well as NCAA sights posting results.

Why should users care about the focus of the site?

Users will care about this site because it is a NCAA focused site and there will not be other info about elite or developmental gymnastics.

Why should a site be built for this?

A sight should be built for this so it is simple for users to read and access quickly, with minimal ads and no subscription required.

What would you like users to do at your site?

I would like users to be able to access meet results and news stories about their favorite college gymnastics teams.

What is the goal of the site?

The goal for this sight is to provide a space where college gymnastics is the star and main focus point.
